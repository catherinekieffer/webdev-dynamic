Our History

Founded in 2006, Airline Safety Informant is a pioneering platform committed to ensuring your safety while traveling in the skies. We are here to serve as your trusted guide, offering in-depth safety data, insightful analyses, and the latest safety-related news in the aviation industry.

Mission Statement

Our mission is rooted in a steadfast dedication to the principles of transparency, accuracy, and accountability. We firmly believe that every traveler has the right to be informed about the safety records and practices of the airlines they entrust their journeys to. At Airline Safety Informant, our unwavering commitment to safety and ethics drives us to provide comprehensive, up-to-date, and impartial information on airline safety. We strive to empower travelers with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions, ensuring that your journey is not just comfortable and convenient but, most importantly, safe. Your safety is our priority, and our dedication to excellence is what sets us apart in the world of aviation safety.
